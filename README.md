## 🚀 About Me

I am a Master's student specializing in **Artificial Intelligence**, with a strong focus on **Computer Vision** and **Machine Learning**.

My current research is conducted within the **Conecta 2030** project, where I work on:

* 🚗 3D Object Detection
* 🎯 Object Tracking
* 📈 Trajectory Prediction
* 🛣️ Traffic Safety Applications

My MSc research proposes a **novel background filtering method for point clouds**, aiming to improve both **accuracy and computational efficiency** in 3D object detection models.

In addition, I contribute to the **LaSID RecSys** group, where I research and implement:

* 🔁 Incremental Recommender Systems
* 🎰 Multi-Armed Bandits
* ⚡ Adaptive and online learning approaches
